Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Welcome to Student Hub. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you interact with our platform, specifically when using external authentication options like "Continue with Google".

1. Information Shared by google.com

To facilitate a seamless sign-in experience, when you click "Continue with Google", google.com will share the following profile details with our site:

  • Name: Your Google account display name, which is used to personalize your student profile within our app.
  • Email Address: Your Google email address, which is used as your unique scholastic log ID.
  • Profile Picture: Your Google public avatar, used to represent your profile on active dashboards.

2. How We Use This Information

The information received from Google is utilized exclusively for:

  • Creating or mapping your academic profile and credentials.
  • Permitting secure, credentialed access to course materials, learning tracker pages, and references.
  • Customizing the Student Hub user interface based on your account settings.

3. Information Protection & Consent

We value your data privacy. We do not sell, license, or share your profile details with external advertising agencies or unrelated third parties. Any user data stored in our databases is protected with secure standard encryption and storage practices.

4. Data Retention and Account Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may delete your account and clear all related academic records, credentials, and progress tracking at any time directly through the Settings dropdown in your Account dashboard.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact the Student Hub project coordinators.
